The Four Winds Around Your Table

American Mahjong is played by four players, each representing one of the cardinal wind directions.

East - The dealer and starting position
South - Sits to East’s right
West - Sits across from East
North - Sits to East’s left

Fun Fact: The wind seating doesn’t follow a traditional compass layout. In Mahjong, the winds follow Asian cardinal points and have traditional associations with seasons (East/Spring, South/Summer, West/Autumn, North/Winter), which may explain why the arrangement differs from a standard compass.

The wind positions rotate throughout the game, giving each player a turn as the dealer. Don’t worry about memorizing everything now, we’ll cover the significance of these positions as we progress through the lessons.
