The Charleston

After the tiles are dealt, the game begins with a Charleston - a series of tile exchanges between players. Each Charleston consists of three passes, with each player passing exactly 3 tiles during each pass.

The final pass of a Charleston can be a blind pass. Instead of selecting 3 tiles from your hand, you can choose 0, 1, or 2 tiles from your own collection and complete the set of 3 by including tiles you just received from another player (without looking at them first).

If all players agree, you can complete two full Charlestons before starting the main game. The order of the passes are:

First Charleston: Right → Across → Left
Second Charleston: Left → Across → Right

After the Charleston(s), players may make an optional courtesy pass with the player sitting across from them. Both players must agree on how many tiles to exchange (0-3 tiles each). Either player can decline to pass any tiles.
